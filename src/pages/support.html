Cr1pt0 Market support

What do you need help with?

  • What is the purpose of this website?
    This website facilitates the trading of in-game items using blockchain technology, providing a secure and transparent platform for users to exchange items.
  • How does blockchain technology enhance item trading?
    Blockchain ensures transparent and tamper-proof transactions. Each item trade is recorded on the blockchain, providing a decentralized and secure ledger for all participants.
  • Is there a fee for using the trading platform?
    Yes, there might be a small transaction fee associated with using our platform. This fee helps maintain and improve the services we provide, ensuring a smooth trading experience.
  • How can I deposit items for trading?
    To deposit items, log in to your account, navigate to the deposit section, and follow the provided instructions. Ensure that you comply with the item eligibility criteria for successful deposits.
  • What security measures are in place to protect my items?
    We employ robust security protocols, including encryption and multi-factor authentication, to safeguard your items. Additionally, the use of blockchain ensures the integrity of transactions and ownership.
  • Can I withdraw real currency from the platform?
    At the moment, our platform focuses on in-game item trading. We do not support direct withdrawal of real currency. You can, however, trade your items for other valuable in-game items.
